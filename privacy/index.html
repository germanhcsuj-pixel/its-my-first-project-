Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Solifon AI respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can control your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • AI-generated content, such as ideas, scripts, captions, hashtags, and posting plans;
  • usage data, such as pages visited and features used;
  • subscription and payment status, if paid features are used.

2. TikTok Data

If you sign in with TikTok, Solifon AI may receive basic profile information and permissions that you approve through TikTok OAuth. We do not collect, ask for, or store your TikTok password.

TikTok permissions are controlled by the user and TikTok. You may revoke access through your TikTok account settings where available.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • provide account login and authentication;
  • generate content ideas, scripts, captions, hashtags, and posting plans;

  • prevent abuse, spam, and unauthorized activity;

4. Content Posting

If TikTok Content Posting API access is approved, Solifon AI will only upload or publish content after you choose and confirm that action inside the app. We do not post to your TikTok account without your confirmation.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate Solifon AI, such as hosting, authentication, payment processing, analytics, security, and AI service providers.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of Solifon AI, users, or others.
