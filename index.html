What Solifon AI does

Solifon AI is built to help creators plan and prepare content faster, while keeping user permissions clear and secure.

Video ideas

Generate TikTok content ideas based on a creator’s topic, audience, and goals.

Scripts and captions

Create short video scripts, hooks, captions, and hashtags for TikTok posts.

Posting plans

Organize content ideas into a simple posting plan for consistent publishing.

Solifon AI does not collect TikTok passwords, does not automate spam, and does not perform fake engagement such as auto-likes, auto-follows, or artificial comments.

TikTok integration

Solifon AI may use TikTok Login Kit to let users sign in securely through TikTok OAuth. If Content Posting API is approved, users will be able to upload or publish content only after they choose and confirm that action.

The user remains responsible for reviewing all AI-generated content and making sure it follows TikTok policies and local laws.
